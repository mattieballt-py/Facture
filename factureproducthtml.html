Product Description

Experience premium sound quality with our Wireless Bluetooth Headphones. Designed for comfort and convenience, these headphones feature the latest Bluetooth 5.0 technology for a stable connection and Active Noise Cancellation to block out unwanted noise.

With up to 30 hours of playtime, you can enjoy your favorite music all day long without worrying about battery life. The built-in microphone allows for hands-free calling, making these headphones perfect for work and play.

Advanced Features

The advanced Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) technology uses external microphones to detect ambient noise and generates inverse sound waves to cancel it out. This gives you an immersive listening experience, even in noisy environments.

Quick charging capability means you'll never be without your music for long. Just 10 minutes of charging gives you 3 hours of playtime, and a full charge provides up to 30 hours of continuous playback.

Comfort & Design

Designed with comfort in mind, these headphones feature soft protein leather earpads and an adjustable headband for a perfect fit. The lightweight design makes them comfortable to wear for extended periods, whether you're commuting, working, or relaxing.
